## 🎮 How to Play

SplitDuel is a 5-round tactical strategy game built on Celo. Here is the breakdown:

1.  **Stake to Enter:** Both players deposit an equal amount of CELO or supported stablecoins into the `DuelManager`. **Your deposit is always protected.**
2.  **Allocate Your Energy:** Every round, you have 60 seconds to secretly split your 100% energy across three vaults:
    *   💥 **ATTACK:** Designed to crush your opponent's defenses.
    *   🛡️ **DEFEND:** Shields your hard-earned gains.
    *   💰 **INVEST:** Focuses entirely on maximizing your yield for the round.
3.  **The Clash:** Choices are revealed simultaneously. Did you attack while they invested? You steal their yield! Did you both attack? It's a bloody clash!
4.  **Victory:** After 5 intense rounds, the player with the highest treasury score claims the accumulated yield. Everyone gets their initial stake back.

## 🚀 Epic Features

*   **Zero Risk to Principal:** You only fight for the yield. You never lose your initial deposit.
*   **Commit-Reveal Mechanics:** True strategic depth. No front-running, just pure mind games.
*   **Daily Split Pool:** Don't want to 1v1? Jump into the daily multiplayer tournament. The top 10% most efficient players share the daily yield pool!
*   **Reputation System:** The more you battle, the higher you climb. Earn permanent efficiency buffs as you rank up from Novice to Legend.
*   **Mobile Ready:** Built specifically for MiniPay with ultra-fast, 60-second rounds.
